Welcome to Peconic Chronic ! We have been around for 20 years even though we are technically new. We use the General hydroponics line of nutrients at all phases of plant life. All plants are grown in a promix medium and we use a combination of 600 watt high pressure sodium bulbs and ceramic metal halide to keep the spectrum as close to the sun as possible. We are everyday smokers that want a safe product and a lot of variety and quality, this is our passion as well as our dream.
